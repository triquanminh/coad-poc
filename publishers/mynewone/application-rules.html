Mynewone Application Rules

Latest update: 23/4/2025

These rules help ensure that Mynewone remains a safe, welcoming, and productive space for everyone. By using our services, you agree to follow these rules.

1. RESPECT OTHERS

Treat all users with respect and kindness. We do not tolerate:

  • Harassment, bullying, or intimidation
  • Hate speech or discrimination based on race, gender, religion, nationality, disability, or sexual orientation
  • Personal attacks or threats of violence
  • Doxxing or sharing personal information without consent

2. AUTHENTIC CONTENT

Share genuine, original content and be honest about who you are:

  • Do not impersonate others or create fake accounts
  • Do not spread misinformation or false news
  • Give credit when sharing others' work
  • Do not manipulate votes or engagement artificially

3. APPROPRIATE CONTENT

Keep content appropriate for our diverse community:

  • No explicit sexual content or nudity
  • No graphic violence or gore
  • No content promoting illegal activities
  • No spam or excessive self-promotion

4. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

Respect intellectual property rights:

  • Do not share copyrighted content without permission
  • Respect trademarks and brand rights
  • Report copyright violations when you see them

5. PRIVACY AND SAFETY

Protect your privacy and that of others:

  • Do not share personal information publicly
  • Report suspicious or harmful behavior
  • Use privacy settings to control who sees your content
  • Be cautious when meeting people from online

6. CONSEQUENCES

Violations of these rules may result in:

  • Content removal
  • Temporary account suspension
  • Permanent account ban
  • Reporting to law enforcement (for illegal activities)

7. REPORTING VIOLATIONS

If you see content or behavior that violates these rules, please report it using our reporting tools or contact our support team at support@mynewone.com.

8. APPEALS

If you believe your content was removed or your account was suspended in error, you can appeal the decision by contacting our support team with details about your case.

9. UPDATES TO RULES

We may update these rules from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes and the updated rules will be posted on this page.

Remember: These rules are in addition to our User Agreement and Privacy Policy. By using Mynewone, you agree to follow all of our terms and policies.
